Red Fort

1: Introduction

The Red Fort is one of the most famous historical monuments in India. It is known for its massive red sandstone walls and beautiful Mughal architecture.

It is located in Delhi, the capital city of India. The fort was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in the 17th century.

Page 2: History & Importance

The construction of the Red Fort was completed in 1648. It served as the main residence of Mughal emperors for nearly 200 years.

The Red Fort is historically important because it represents India's rich culture and heritage. Every year on Independence Day, the Prime Minister hoists the national flag here.

Interesting Facts

  • Built using red sandstone
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Residence of Mughal emperors
  • Independence Day celebration place
